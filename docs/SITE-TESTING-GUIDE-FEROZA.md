## 8. PHONE/MOBILE TESTING

Open the site on your phone and check:

- [ ] Text is readable (not too small or too large)

## 9. THINGS TO LOOK OUT FOR

- Any page that shows a **blank white screen** — note the URL
- Any **broken images** (grey box or broken icon instead of a photo)
- Any link that goes to a **"Page not found"** or **404** error
- Any text that looks **wrong, misspelled, or outdated**
- Anything that looks **different on phone vs computer**
